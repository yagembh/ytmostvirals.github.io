Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

1. Introduction

ViralFlow is a content tool that finds the hottest gaming moments and publishes them to social media platforms on behalf of the account owner.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share personal data from any third-party users. The only information used by the app is social media account credentials provided by the account owner (stored locally, never transmitted to third parties) and video metadata from YouTube (public data via YouTube Data API v3).

3. How We Use Information

Account credentials are used solely to authenticate with social media platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) and publish content on behalf of the account owner. No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

ViralFlow integrates with YouTube Data API v3 (Google) and TikTok Content Posting API. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data. All credentials are stored locally on the account owner's device.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
